The public transport trap with children

Many families think about taking the RER A to save money. Here's the reality:

  • Packed carriages — on weekends and during school holidays, it's chaos. Your children standing for 45 minutes, squeezed between passengers
  • Pushchair impossible — stairs without lifts, narrow turnstiles, endless connections
  • Pickpockets — families loaded with bags and suitcases are known targets on the RER
  • Children exhausted before even arriving — 60-70 minutes in reality (walking, waiting, connections)
  • No child seats — your children travel standing or on your lap

Result: you arrive at Disneyland exhausted, stressed and late. Not the magical start you had in mind.
